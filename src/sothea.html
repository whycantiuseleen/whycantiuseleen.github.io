THE PROJECT

BACKGROUND

Due to the political unrest in Cambodia, adminstration of the national record was not properly done during those periods and hence there is a lack of centralised national records for identification. This makes the medical health screenings in Cambodian villages highly inefficient as numerous man hours are often wasted on the retrieval of medical histories as well as on identifying both new and revisiting patients. To alleviate this problem, a medical database was set up by SUTD Makerspace with an accompanying web application for the NUS medical students who provides free health screenings for the Cambodians annually.

However, there were negative feedbacks from the medical students regarding the web application and hence the team will be looking into redesigning this web application to help ease their pain points.

GOALS

  • Reduce the time taken for the Medical Screenings
  • Increase Doctor-Patient Interaction during the Doctor's Consultation
  • Reduce User Cognitive Load

PROCESS

01 DISCOVER

To understand about our user's pain-points, we have gathered their feedback and also analyzed the existing user interface.

"Cambodian patients usually have very short-attention span and hence there are many that dropped out halfway through the screening since it takes too long for us to screen them and fill in the forms."

- NUS Medical Student

"The interface is messy and unintuitive as information is all over the place. It takes too long for us to obtain the information for diagnosis to be made."

- NUS Medical Student

Areas for improvements were identified in the existing UI which includes the following:

  • Possible user-driven error
    e.g. clear-all button is currently placed right beside the submit button
  • Graphic design concerns: proper alignment, colors and rearrangement of field
  • Prioritizing of information

    • 02 ANALYSIS

    Since there were feedback regarding the dropouts during the screening process due to the short-attention span that the Cambodians have, one of our project goals was hence to shorten the total time taken for the whole screening process. This means that we had to focus on shortening the time taken for the form-filling and to increase Doctor-Patient interaction so that the patients will feel engaged in the session itself. We also aimed to reduce cognitive load for the users, allowing them to focus better for each session.

    Two different user flows were identified for this project:

  • Medical staffs who are assigned to the 4 different medical stations
  • Doctors who are in charge of carrying out the consultation sessions.
