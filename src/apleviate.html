THE PROJECT

BACKGROUND

Aerial Defence is an interactive multiplayer game developed by taking inspiration from the 1930s pencil and paper game “The Battleships”. It is a digital remake with the introduction of role-playing characters that is designed to hold unique abilities and it is also a competitive multiplayer turn based game.

GOALS

  • Introduce strategic thinking into the original luck-based game.
  • Intuitive game design new players to easily assimilate into the game.

PROCESS

01 IDEATION

Since the original "Battleships" was mainly a luck-based game, the team wanted to remake the game in order to introduce strategic thinking elements so as to make the game more diverse and exciting for the players. We wanted to provide game mechanics that will allow players to have a totally new experience for each round of gameplay as they will have to weigh the pros and cons of each action that is taken.

From there, we decided to develop a RPG which will introduce randomness and hence multiple approaches towards each round. Each character that was introduced into the game has specific roles and abilities that will significantly turn the tables at any one point.
